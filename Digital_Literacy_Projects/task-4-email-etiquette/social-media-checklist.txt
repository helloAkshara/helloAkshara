Social Media Do’s & Don’ts Checklist

DO’s:
* Maintain a professional profile (clear bio, appropriate photo).
* Think before posting content.
* Respect others’ opinions and maintain polite communication.
* Use privacy settings wisely.
* Share meaningful and positive content.
* Credit original creators when reposting.

DON’Ts:
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
* Do not share personal/sensitive information publicly.
* Avoid engaging in online arguments or hate speech.
* Do not spread misinformation or unverified news.
* Avoid excessive posting or spamming.
* Don’t use unprofessional language in public posts.
